I'm Stefano Riva, born in Milan in 1997.

I attended *Liceo Scientifico* at *Salesiani Sesto* (E. Breda), I obtained a MSc degree in Nuclear Engineering *cum Laude* at [Politecnico di Milano](https://polimi.it). From May 2022, I'm a PhD student in *Energy and Nuclear Science and Engineering* at the [Nuclear Reactors Group](https://www.nuclearenergy.polimi.it) of the Department of Energy at Politecnico di Milano, under the supervision of professor Antonio Cammi. 

My research interests are about:

- Reduced Order Modelling
- Computational Fluid Dynamics
- Multi-physics simulations of nuclear reactors
