In this research, the development of Reduced Order Models (ROM) for multi-physic systems is investigated; in particular, a lot of efforts are concentrated in integrating mathematical models and experimental data in a Data Assimilation framework. The general scheme of this approach is the following:

<img src='/images/research/ROM_DA_scheme.png' alt=''>

The measurements are conceived as an update with respect to the prediction of the mathematical model, they include further information of the coupling between differenet variables, which is usually approximated in the modelling.
The role of model order reduction is fundamental to reduce the computational burden of the numerical solution of *High-Fidelity* models.
